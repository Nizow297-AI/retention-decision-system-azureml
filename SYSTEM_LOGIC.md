# System Logic

## Step 1: Predict Churn
Model assigns a probability of churn to each customer.

## Step 2: Segment Risk
Customers are grouped into:
- High risk
- Medium risk
- Low risk

## Step 3: Select Strategy
Based on A/B testing:
- Strategy A performs best

## Step 4: Final Decision
- High-risk customers → Strategy A
- Others → No action

## Summary
This system combines predictive modeling and experimentation to support retention decisions.
